OET: Tailored Language Proficiency for Healthcare Professionals

The Occupational English Test (OET) is meticulously designed to cater to the specific language requirements of healthcare practitioners aspiring to work in English-speaking environments.

  • Comprehensive Assessment
  • Global Acceptance
  • Ownership and Partnership

Comprehensive Assessment

OET evaluates the language proficiency of healthcare professionals across all four language skills, with Writing and Speaking tests tailored to 12 distinct healthcare areas. Meanwhile, all candidates undergo the same Reading and Listening tests.

Specialized Testing: OET recognizes the unique language demands of various healthcare fields, ensuring that professionals are adequately prepared for their specific roles.

Global Acceptance

OET serves as a credible proof of English proficiency and is recognized in several countries, including the UK, Ireland, Australia, New Zealand, Dubai, Namibia, and Singapore. It is widely accepted for registration in 12 different healthcare professions.

Global Opportunities: Healthcare professionals proficient in OET can pursue their careers in diverse English-speaking regions, expanding their horizons.

Industry Standard: The acceptance of OET underscores its status as a trusted language assessment tool for the healthcare sector.

Ownership and Partnership

OET is owned by the Cambridge Boxhill Language Assessment Trust (CBLA), a collaborative venture between Cambridge English and Box Hill Institute.

Cambridge English Expertise: CBLA's affiliation with Cambridge English reinforces the test's adherence to rigorous language assessment standards.

Educational Partnership: Box Hill Institute's involvement ensures that OET aligns with the educational needs of healthcare professionals.
